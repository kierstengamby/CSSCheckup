Green Thumb

Here are a few ways to increase your environmental consciousness:

  • Recycle: make sure you check with your local recycling plant to see which items can be recycled in your area.
  • Conserve water: when you're not using it, turn off the tap.
  • Unplug your electronics: only leave chords plugged in when you are actively charging something.
  • Plant a tree: to help with the overall ecosystem and stop deforestation.
  • Rewear your clothes: don't buy into fast-fashion, rewearing clothes saves landfills from piling up.
  • Compost: your unconsumed food doesn't have to go to waste - turn it into dirt.
